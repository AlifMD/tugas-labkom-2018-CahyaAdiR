What is the dangers of ACETONE?

Acetone is a clear, colorless liquid that is an extremely flammable chemical. Acetone is a common item, but it can make some serious risks. When a person uses acetone and inhale the acetone smell, it will indirectly cause lungs irritation. For short-term, if often inhaled can make headache, irritation of the eyes, and nose. For the long-term can damage the nervou s in the nose, throat, and brain. The long term can make our skin dry, cracks and also damage the nerveous system. Explosion can happen when acetone is mixed with hydrogen peroxide, nitric acid and sulfur dichloride.To avoid occupational safety hazards, we must know what chemicals to segregate from acetone. To throw away the liquid acetone, you need to take the acetone to a hazardous waste treatment.
